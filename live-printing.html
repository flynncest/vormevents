Service 01

Live Printing

Full-colour digital printing on canvas tote bags, pouches, and fabric items — done live at your stand while they watch. Bold, vivid, and impossible to throw away. High throughput, zero mess, and a keepsake that goes everywhere they go.

What You Get

Four outcomes. One activation.

01

Full-Colour at Your Stand

Not a stamp — a full photographic-quality print. Your logo in every colour, sharp edges, vivid on canvas. The visual contrast at your stand is immediate: while everyone else has a banner, you have a craft station people stop to watch.

02

Items They Use Every Day

A canvas tote goes to the supermarket, the gym, the airport. Every trip out is another impression. Your brand on something people reach for — not something they leave in a conference bag and forget.

03

High Throughput, No Waiting

Live printing runs at 15–20 items per hour. For high-footfall events, a second printer can be deployed to double output without sacrificing quality or the experience of watching it happen.

04

Personalise the Moment

Add the attendee's name, initials, or a custom message alongside your logo. Each item is made for them, in front of them — and that's exactly what makes them keep it and show it to people.

Our Materials

What we print on

Canvas

Heavyweight woven canvas — structured enough to print on with precision, durable enough to carry daily. Colour sits vivid and permanent on natural or dyed canvas.

  • Tote bags, shoppers, backpacks, drawstring bags
  • Full-colour digital print or single-colour logo

Cotton

Soft, everyday cotton — the material people reach for. Prints anchor to the fibres for a lasting, wash-resistant finish that doesn't crack or peel over time.

  • Tote bags, t-shirts, pouches, cotton accessories
  • Full-colour digital print

Mixed Fabric

Polyester blends and technical fabrics for event merchandise that goes beyond the office — caps, sport bags, and functional accessories with lasting print quality.

  • Caps, sports bags, functional accessories
  • Sublimation or direct-to-film print

Our live printing station operates at 15–20 personalised items per hour. This allows time for personalisation, quality checking, and the all-important moment of watching it print live.
