Event Services

Live hot foil stamping
at your event

Hot foil stamping is a centuries-old craft — a heated metal die presses through a thin layer of metallic foil, leaving a permanent, tactile impression in gold, colour, or blind press. We bring this process live to your event floor, personalising leather journals, cork totes, and canvas pouches in real time. The result: a stand people queue for, leads you capture while they wait, and a branded keepsake every attendee takes home.

Our Materials

What we stamp on

Vegan Leather (PU)

  • Gold foil, colour foil, or pressed lettering

Common Questions

Everything you need to know

Hot foil stamping uses the traditional technique of heat embossing — a manual press stamps a design (your logo, a name, initials) onto items at your event using a heated metal die. The die presses through a thin foil film onto the surface, leaving a permanent, tactile impression in gold, colour, or blind press. It's done live on-site, giving attendees an interactive experience they can watch and take home.

Our trained stamping artisans operate professional-grade hot foil embossing machines with speed and precision. They handle everything on the day — setup, personalisation, queue management, and breakdown — so your team can focus entirely on conversations and lead capture.

One artisan can personalise one item approximately every 1–1.5 minutes — equating to 40–50 items per hour. This includes logo foiling, individual letter stamping (name or initials), item handling, and quality checking. Additional artisans and machines can be added to increase capacity proportionally for larger events.

We specialise in quality vegan leather (PU) accessories including notebooks, luggage tags, card holders, keyrings, passport holders, mousepads, and laptop sleeves. We also work with natural cork (journals, totes, coasters) and heavyweight canvas (tote bags, pouches, backpacks). Browse our full product range in the catalog.
