Service 02

Live Hot Foil
Stamping

A heated die presses your logo and the attendee's name into a luxury leather, cork, or canvas item — in seconds, in front of them. The result: a stand people queue for, a natural lead-capture moment while they wait, and a branded keepsake that lives on every desk for years. We handle setup, personalisation, and breakdown. You handle the conversations.

What You Get

Four outcomes. One activation.

01

Drive Foot Traffic to Your Stand

Live hot foil stamping is the most visually compelling thing on any show floor. The moment the die comes down and the gold reveals, people stop mid-stride. One person watching becomes ten. Your stand becomes the one everyone asks about — before lunch.

02

Turn Your Brand Into a Daily Impression

Your logo in gold on a leather journal. A canvas tote they take to the airport. A card holder that goes to every meeting. Unlike a lanyard or a brochure, a personalised item doesn't get binned — it stays in use, in view, and in front of the people who matter.

03

Their Name. Stamped Live. Kept Forever.

The 2–3 minute wait is your window. Badge scan, short conversation, contact added. Just warm leads captured while they're smiling — no chasing required after the event.

04

Earn Genuine Attendee Goodwill

There's a difference between receiving a freebie and receiving a gift. An item made live, in front of them, with their name stamped in gold — that's a gift. It creates the kind of genuine goodwill a follow-up email can't manufacture.

Our Materials

What we stamp on

Vegan Leather (PU)

Premium PU leather — the look and feel of genuine leather, without compromise. Holds a hot foil impression with clean edges and lasting depth.

  • Journals, card cases, passport holders, luggage tags
  • Gold foil, colour foil, or blind press

Cork

Sustainable, tactile, and visually distinct. The foil cuts a clean impression into the cork grain — warm, considered, and luxurious.

  • Journals, tote bags, coasters, luggage tags
  • Gold or blind emboss — colour foil available

Canvas

Heavyweight canvas elevated from merchandise to memento. Your logo stamped in gold on something they carry every day.

  • Tote bags, backpacks, pouches, pencil cases
  • Gold foil, colour foil, or pressed lettering

The Personalisation

More than a logo — a name

Beyond your brand mark, every item can be personalised with the attendee's own name or initials — stamped live, in front of them using individual metal type characters arranged and pressed by hand.

The result is something they can't get anywhere else: your brand logo and their name, side by side, on a luxury material they chose.
