About Monospace Events

Craftsmanship meets event ROI

Monospace Events is a live hot foil stamping company built for the corporate event world. We exist because branded merchandise has a problem: most of it gets binned within 24 hours of the event.

A leather item personalised live, with the attendee's name in gold alongside your brand mark — doesn't get binned. It goes home, sits on the desk, and travels to every meeting they attend. That's the brand impression we deliver at trade shows, conferences, and product launches across Europe.
